Experience

As a technical professional with over 14 years of experience in Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) operations, I specialize in Weapons of Mass Destruction (WMD), Improvised Explosive Devices (IED), and Homemade Explosives (HME). Throughout my career, I have conducted classroom presentations related to basic explosive ordnance and decontamination awareness.

My experience includes 11 years overseas with 4.5 years in Afghanistan as an Unexploded Ordnance Disposal Technician (UXO Technician II) and 7 years in Iraq as EOD Technician for the Department of State. During my time in Afghanistan, I successfully developed and implemented plans to account for and safely dispose of unserviceable explosive ordnance. In Iraq, I conducted post-blast analysis training to Iraqi Army EOD and Iraqi Police Bomb Squad departments.

I have a proven track record of leadership and team building, as well as expertise in complex WMD and IED operations, policy and program development, and strategic planning and execution. Additionally, I am well-versed in regulatory compliance, chemical weapons training, biological threat reduction, and nonproliferation prevention.
