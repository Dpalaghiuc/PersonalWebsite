About

Four years ago, I embarked on a journey to transition from Explosive Ordnance Disposal to the dynamic world of technology. While working full-time in Iraq, I am simultaneously pursuing my Bachelor's degree at Harvard Extension School. This institution provides me with a high-quality education, thanks to its exceptional instructors and diverse cohort of classmates.

I opted for the Computer Science (CS) program at Harvard Extension School because it allows students to follow the same academic semester roadmap as those at Harvard College. Having access to the same courses as Harvard College students is an invaluable opportunity that enriches my learning experience.

Harvard Extension
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science

As of now, my academic journey has me set to graduate with my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Harvard Extension School in December 2023. The robust curriculum and the supportive learning environment have greatly enriched my understanding of the subject. I am confident that this undergraduate degree will equip me with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the technology industry. This degree will also provide a solid foundation for my future academic and professional pursuits.

Carnegie Mellon University
Master's degree Information Security and Assurance

Post my undergraduate studies, I plan to advance my expertise by enrolling in the prestigious Master's program in Information Security and Assurance at Carnegie Mellon University. This program is renowned for its comprehensive curriculum that perfectly blends risk management, information security, and data privacy. I am particularly drawn to the flexibility of the program that will allow me to balance my professional and personal commitments alongside my academics. I am excited about the opportunity to broaden my cybersecurity knowledge and further hone my technology skills.

Department of State

On the professional front, I aspire to become a Foreign Service Information Management Specialist for the U.S. Department of State.
